Insider’s InOne platform brings together predictive marketing and real-time technologies marketers need to deliver personalized experiences under a single, highly usable platform. InOne empowers marketers across industries to deliver individualized and consistent customer experiences across web, mobile web and apps, without depending on a team of data scientists and IT teams.

Powered by predictive modelling, machine learning and AI algorithms, InOne collects, unifies and mines data, providing ready-to-use segments to marketers. At the intersection of adtech and martech, InOne helps marketers go beyond conversion optimization and helps them increase marketing spend efficiency.
